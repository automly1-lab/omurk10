Lead Operations Systems

Turn scattered lead work into a clear pipeline of next actions.

Research, qualification, outreach, replies, and follow-ups often live across too many tabs. Crestline Ops builds systems that help teams find the right prospects, keep records clean, and know what should happen next.

Research

Collect company details, websites, contact channels, location, service fit, and notes from approved sources.

Qualify

Score leads by fit, available contact path, relevance, risk flags, and whether a human should review before outreach.

Track replies

Replies can be logged, classified, and surfaced so interested prospects do not get buried in the inbox.

Designed for careful outreach

The system can support slower sending, review steps, do-not-contact lists, reply alerts, and clear logs. The goal is to help the team stay consistent without making the outreach feel careless or mass-produced.
