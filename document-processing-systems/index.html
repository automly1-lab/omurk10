Document Processing Systems

Turn messy documents into clean operational records.

PDFs, spreadsheets, forms, and inbox attachments often hold the data teams need, but the manual copying creates delays and mistakes. Crestline Ops builds practical document systems that extract the important fields, flag uncertain values, and send clean records to the tools your team already uses.

Read the source

Incoming PDFs, spreadsheets, and attachments can be collected from email, Drive folders, forms, or shared work queues.

Structure the data

Names, dates, prices, addresses, terms, totals, notes, and custom fields can be extracted into consistent columns.

Review exceptions

Missing values, low-confidence fields, and unusual formats can be sent to a review queue instead of being guessed silently.

Common workflows

Lease documents, sale comparables, invoices, owner reports, onboarding forms, applications, contracts, and operational spreadsheets can all be turned into clean rows for review.

Where the data can land

Records can be written to Google Sheets, CRM fields, review dashboards, task lists, email summaries, or internal trackers depending on how your team works today.
